About Me

I am a highly motivated and analytical student with a strong foundation in mathematics, physics, and computer science. I enjoy working close to the hardware layer, using C++ and microcontrollers to build systems that interact with the real world.

My interests include embedded firmware, low-level debugging, and turning boards like the Arduino and ESP32 into reliable devices. I pay attention to clean, readable code and like documenting my work so that teammates and my future self can understand it easily.

Having studied in an international environment and speaking Korean, English, and French, I am comfortable collaborating across cultures and accessing technical resources in multiple languages.
